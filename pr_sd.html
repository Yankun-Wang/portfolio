Problem Roulette

Problem Roulette (PR) is a point-free study service that offers topical access to a library of locally-authored exam problems in selected foundational courses at the University of Michigan.

Problem Overview

How to increase the visit rate of the dashboard page?

On Problem Roulette, the dashboard page allows student users to view their activity data on the platform and the questions they saved during their student sessions. However, according to our internal data, the visit rate of the dashboard page was relatively low.
