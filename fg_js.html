SAP Fieldglass Job Seeker Preview Modal Redesign

Redesign the Job Seeker Preview Modal to improve the user experience of reviewing job candidates

Goals
  • Improve the experience of reviewing candidates to help users make informed decisions
  • Assist the pre-sales team to attract more potential clients
Role

I sole designed and tested the redesign of the widget and worked with Pre-Sale team, PMs, developers and QA team to craft a better home page experience for the users.

Introduction

Improve the Candidate Reviewing experience on SAP Fieldglass
