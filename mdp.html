Mission Coupler

A communications tool capable of data tracking for mission-driven organizations, improving communication with their community

Project Type

Team project for Multidisciplinary Design Program

Team

Sofia Alidina (PM), Sean Anggani (front-end), Arun Massand (front-end), Nick McKay (back-end), Calvin Shenk (back-end), Yankun Wang (UXD), Eugene Yoon (UXR)

Duration

Winter 2019 - Fall 2019 (6 months)

Role

UX Designer, UX Researcher, Visual Designer

Problem

Problem of Mission-driven Organizations

Many mission-driven organizations have difficulty maintaining consistent and reliable communication with clients during and after services are provided, which makes it challenging to understand evolving community needs and maintain impact metrics.

Communication

Inefficient or ineffective outreach and communications creates challenges reaching end-users

Demonstrate Impact

Difficulty reaching end-users impedes program improvement and impact evaluation

Obtain Funding

Insufficient impact data impedes additional funding

Design Overview

Customizable web-based application

Mission Coupler is a customizable web-based application which Integrates external communication services( survey - LimeSurvey, text message - Trillo), collecting data that demonstrate organizations’ impact, understand evolving community needs for providing better services and obtaining future fundings.

1. Dashboard

The dashboard displays rich data that indicates the impact of the mission-driven organization, providing quantification of impact while obtaining fundings

2. Organize Surveys

The survey panel allows users to organize the surveys from the survey provider(LimeSurvey). Organizations can send out surveys using text message and capture data using API

3. Manage member

Users can edit, search and sort the member list using the app. Users can either upload an existing CSV file to add new members or manually input information. Users can also create member groups according to different programs or services.

4. Schedule & Direct message

Schedule view helps users visualize ongoing and future survey activities. Users can also set up routine surveys using the scheduling tool. Besides, users can send a text message to a certain member for direct communication.

User Research

Interviews & Observation

I collaborated with the researchers in the team to understand our core users, their environment, and majors tasks to accomplish. We conducted 5 semi-structured interviews with mission-driven organizations and gathered data on their current workflow and major challenges. This data helped us in finding opportunities to solve the problems.

Participants

Staffs related to data management from different organizations

Interviews Script

1. What's the context?
2. What are your major tasks?
3. How do you report & analyze data?
4. What are the major problems faced?
5. How do collaborations happen?
6. What tools do you currently use?

Observations

Observations were conducted to get and understanding the data collection process of different organizations

Affinity Diagram

We went through our interviews and extracted discrete ideas or direct quotes that represented intact pieces of qualitative data. We then organized these notes into an affinity diagram to see the most relevant issues.

Reflection

Create a focused minimum viable product (MVP)

In this project, we are a relatively small team without much industry experience. It’s important for our team to narrow down the target audience and focus on the problem we are capable to solve. Our stakeholder came with a ambiguous project scope and we had to focus on being agile, work in small increments and aim to move fast. Although we discovered additional problems through stakeholder conversations, we reconsidered the overall impact on all the problems and tried to define an effective solution.
